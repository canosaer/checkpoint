Checkpoint!

Checkpoint is a game inspired by the indie hit, "Papers, Please." In checkpoint, you assume the role of a border agent in the fictional Eastern European country of Obristan circa 1982. Each round, an entrant will arrive at the border checkpoint and you will have 60 seconds to read what they have to say at the checkpoint, examine their passport, consult reference material, and decide whether to approve or deny their admission to Obristan.

When deciding whether to approve or deny admission, be sure to read what they have to say and check the details on their passport. The reference section includes today's date to check against the expiration date on their passport, as well as valid passport issuing cities for Obristan and the neighboring countries of Kolechia and Antegria.

When you're ready, click the button below to begin. You'll have 60 seconds to decide whether to approve or deny the first entrant!
